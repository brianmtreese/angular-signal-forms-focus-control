Why We Need Your Address

Your billing address is essential for verifying your identity and ensuring secure transactions. Financial institutions use address verification as part of their fraud prevention measures, which helps protect both you and us from unauthorized charges. Additionally, accurate address information ensures that any important account communications or documents reach you promptly.

If you're purchasing a physical product, we'll use this address to calculate shipping costs and delivery times. Our system automatically checks available shipping options based on your location, so providing complete and accurate information helps us give you the most precise estimates. International orders may require additional verification steps, which we'll guide you through during checkout.

Rest assured that your address information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with industry standards. We never share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your order or as required by law. You can update your address at any time through your account settings after completing your purchase.
